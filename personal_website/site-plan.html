Content

Home page

This site has been made for ease the burden of meal choosing and/or planning for everyone. Whether you are a student that has a busy week and sometimes struggles to choose what to eat when your mind is otherwise occupied, or you are a mom just getting home from work to find out that your family has no dinner plans. Random Meal Generator has your back! I am Sean Walker and the creator of this website. I am a college student that is taking classes full time while at the same time, I am working part time to get myself through school with not debt. After all the time and work that I put into school, I sometimes am too tired to try and figure out what I want to eat. This is what motivated me to make this website. It is going to take a weight off my shoulders to not need to worry about choosing what to eat. Now I can use this site to simplify my choice! If we look at the world as it is currently, food is a huge part of our culture. Whether we feel pride for our own local foods or enjoy trying new foods while we travel, food has an impact on who we are. I hope this site can help you love food a little more and help food be part of your life.

Food Generator

This page is where the real magic happens! Using this page, you will be able to really find a solution to indecision. Below is a random number generator. Us the generator and return to the food list page to find what you will make. This is a great option for a date night. If you and your date want to make a nice meal together, why not leave it up to chance. You might be pleasantly surprised to find a new food that you have never made before and a great time trying to learn to make it together. This is also a great option for meal planning. Use the generator and write down what numbers you get for the number of meals you need to make. Return to the food list page and you will be able to write down what your plan is. This is great for many things including saving money, eating healthier, and making mealtimes more exciting. You will be able to save money by only ordering the foods that you need for the meals. You will eat healthier by getting a better variety in your diet. You will have more excitement by eating new and exciting foods!
