Content

Home page

This site has been made for ease the burden of meal choosing and/or planning for everyone. Whether you are a student that has a busy week and sometimes struggles to choose what to eat when you mind is otherwise occupied, or you are a mom just getting home from work to find out that your family has no dinner plans. Random Meal Generator has your back!

Images for the Home page

Recipe List

The recipe list on this page is prepared to help anyone that is indecisive or just struggles to remember more then just a couple of options to cook. Look through the list of meals below and choose whatever you would like to prepare. Maybe you will also find new ideas on this list that you have never tried before. Whether you stick to the norm or try something new, this list will make your choice easier.

Images for the Page 2

Recipe Generator

This page is where the real magic happens! The recipe generator will choose random recipes from the recipe list so that there is no effort needed by the user to choose what to make. Random chance provided by the generator will provide you a night of ease or excitement. If you do not like the recipe chosen, spin it again until you find something you really love.
